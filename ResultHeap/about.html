The History Of ResultHeap

.

In The Beginning...

ResultHeap started out in 2014 as nothing more than a undergraduate college project designed to show how responsive websites could be made quickly using minimal code. The main idea behind creating responsive websites was brought about with our own personal use of mobile devices and how we noticed that some websites did not resize to our screen sizes. We decided that responsive websites were a necesarry attribute.

From this, ResultHeap progressed into the idea of creating responsive and elegant websites that could easily be used on different divices of many different screen sizes. As more and more features and APIs were created, we continued to integrate more powerful technology in order to improve funtionality.

The Here And Now...

Today, ResultHeap has come a long way from being a simple college project. We kept the original concepts of mobility and efficiency, and also continued to apply different frameworks and apps to increase functionality and speed.

We take pride in all our work. Integrating powerful online API's is something we do to improve your website and help grow your business. We keep your site up to date with current trends, helping you to get your name out there while cutting out all the annoying setup and creation process. We make things simple and sweet to make your website experience as easy as possible.
