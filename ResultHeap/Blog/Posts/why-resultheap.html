Why ResultHeap?

Why choose ResultHeap for your web development needs?

June 11, 2020 Isaac Mertzenich

Overview

In this day and age, there are so many different website development and web hosting companies that it seems almost impossible to find the right one with the lowest costs while still getting the quality you need. What seems like a simple Google search quickly becomes a full blown research project just to find what you are looking to get. Plenty of hosting services advertise Wordpress and marketplace integrations, but at a much higher cost than one would want. In fact features like a simple marketplace can cost you upwards of $5,000. If this number sounds high, then you're right. If you own a small business, spending $5,000 just on a marketplace can be far too much of an investment. Even a custom website, without too many bells and whistles, can cost you upwards of $2,000, still a very steep price.

What Makes Our Websites Stand Out?

ResultHeap takes a much different approach, keeping costs lower than all the major companies without sacrificing speed, funcinality, and beauty. Integrations with the most powerful technology on the web (Node.js, Bootstrap, Google and more) are a necessity these days and we use them to our advantage. All websites are responsive and mobile ready, meaning your website will resize and work on any screen size. This can help by drawing people who are more prone to using smartphones and tablets.

What's The Cost?

Of course, everyone wants to know how much our services cost. Because our services vary from client to client, I can't give you an exact number, but our multipage websites for a small business usually don't run more than $1,000. Keep in mind that we include all the source code for your website. With other providers, the cost for such a feature can go even above $5,000, sometimes $10k depending on the company. Adding ecommerce integrations to your site will increase the cost, but this is based on the ammount of transactions, products, and dollar intake you marketplace deals with. This can cost around $500-$1000, depending on the size and funtionality needed.

Conclusion

Everyone wants their website to be cheap, but effective. This is what we do. We want you to be happy with both the product and the customer service and we always strive to give you an amazing experience.
